All about us

Who are we?

We are a small, family-run animal charity based in London whose focus is on caring for unwanted dogs and cats until they can find their forever home.

What do we do?

We look after any animal that comes into our care as if they were our own family, because they are! While we are able to dedicate all the time in the world to looking after our residents, unfortunately this is a very expensive job. We decided to start selling pet gifts so that you can support our work while getting something tangible in return which you can give to your own four-legged friend. Please have a look at our fun toys, and consider buying a present for a cat, or a gift for a dog!
